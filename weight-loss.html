What Is Medical Weight Loss?

Medical weight loss refers to a clinical program developed and supervised by doctors or healthcare professionals. It goes beyond generic meal plans and workout tips, focusing on treating weight issues as a health condition.

At Healthwise, we assess your overall health, conduct lab work if needed, and develop a custom plan that may include prescription weight loss medications, appetite suppressants, or hormone therapy. This approach ensures a safer, more sustainable path to fat loss.

Benefits of Choosing a Medical Weight Loss Program

Choosing a doctor-supervised weight loss program offers several advantages over commercial diet plans. You receive professional support, real medical evaluations, and evidence-based treatment plans that adapt to your progress.

Our clients often see improvements in related conditions like high cholesterol, insulin resistance, and fatigue. More importantly, the program teaches long-term strategies for maintaining a healthy lifestyle, not just short-term weight reduction.

Are You a Candidate for Medical Weight Loss?

If you've tried to lose weight through dieting and exercise with little success, or if your weight is affecting your health, you may benefit from a medical weight loss program. Conditions such as obesity, type 2 diabetes, PCOS, thyroid imbalance, and metabolic syndrome often require medical guidance for effective weight management.
