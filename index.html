What is Medical Weight Loss at HealthWise Medical?

Our medical weight loss is a safe, medically supervised approach that targets the underlying causes of weight gain. Unlike fad diets that offer short-term solutions, our medical weight loss programs use FDA-approved weight loss drugs like GLP-1 medications to promote safe and sustainable results.

Our programs combine GLP-1 weight loss medications, prescription medications, personalized diet and exercise plans, and regular exercise to help you lose weight while improving overall health.

Our Unique Approach to Medical Weight Loss

At HealthWise Medical, our weight loss clinicians go beyond the surface. We start by conducting a thorough health assessment, including blood panel analysis, to understand your body from the inside out.

This includes evaluating the health of your cardiovascular system, metabolism, thyroid, and adrenal glands. By doing so, we can identify any nutrient deficiencies, hormonal imbalances, or organ dysfunction that may be hindering your weight loss efforts.

Long-term Weight Management with GLP-1 Medications

Our focus isn't just on shedding pounds—it's about long-term weight management. By addressing factors like metabolism, lifestyle, and underlying health issues, we ensure lasting success.

For many medications like GLP-1 agonists, play a key role in controlling appetite and boosting metabolic function. Our programs are carefully designed to help you reach your goals and maintain them over time by addressing your body's specific needs, including your blood pressure, heart health, and nutrition.
